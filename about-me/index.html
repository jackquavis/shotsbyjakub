Hello there, I'm Jakub 👋

I love taking concert photos and portraits. My journey in photography began in high school, when I got my first camera. I was itching to learn everything I could and immediately started taking photos for family, friends, and bands. I also exposed myself to nature and architecture photography.

After many years experimenting in the Rutgers University photography club and photographing friends and local bands, I learned that I love photographing people. My favorite part of photography is being able to deliver results that people love. Being able to show raw emotion through a picture makes all the hours of work worth it.

Thank you for taking the time to check out my work. If you're interested in collaborating or want to see more of my work, feel free to reach out. I'd love to work together and help bring your ideas to life. My email and Instagram are always open.
